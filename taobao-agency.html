What is Taobao ?

Taobao is the web site for online shopping, similar to eBay, Rakuten and Amazon operated in China.
Taobao has more than 100 million users, including shops, malls and ordinary people. It's a huge market for buying, as well as selling goods.

How can I benefit using Taobao ?

Using Taobao means buying directly from the supplier.
Prices on Taobao are usually much lower, than in local stores.
You can open your own Taobao store and sell your goods online to Chinese people.

So what's the problem ?

Taobao uses Chinese interface. It means all the words, all product descriptions are written in Chinese characters.
In case you'd like to make a request about the color of the stuff you want to buy, or the quantity, or any other quesiton, you'd have to use Chinese,
as most of Chinese barely speak any English.

The other problem is that you need to set up account to use Taobao. Again, the interface is in Chinese.
To apply for a Taobao account you need to have a Chinese debit/credit card, security flash-card, and only then you can buy online on Taobao.
