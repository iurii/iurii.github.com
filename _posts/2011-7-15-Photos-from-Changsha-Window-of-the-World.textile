p(meta). 15 July 2011 - Shenzhen

I love Changsha. Once I asked someone why people of Changsha don't stare at foreigners. They said there are a lot of country men in Shenzhen, so they stare at you. In Changsha only locals, not much people from the province. And locals are more civilized then country men.
